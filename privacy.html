Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how SunFlex (“SunFlex”, “we”, “us”, or “the App”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the SunFlex application.

Purpose of the App

SunFlex is a companion guidance application designed to provide general, informational recommendations related to sun exposure. The App uses environmental information, such as UV index data, to assist users in making informed decisions.

The App is provided for informational and educational purposes only and is not intended to provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Not Medical Advice

SunFlex does not provide medical advice and should not be relied upon as a substitute for professional medical guidance.

Information presented in the App is general in nature and may not be appropriate for all individuals. Always seek advice from a qualified healthcare professional regarding any medical or skin-related concerns.

Information We Collect

SunFlex collects only the information necessary to provide core functionality. This may include:

  • Approximate location data to display relevant UV index information
  • Basic usage data to improve app performance and reliability

SunFlex does not require users to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable information such as names, physical addresses, or payment details unless explicitly provided by the user.

How We Use Information

Information collected by the App is used solely to:

  • Provide and improve app features
  • Display relevant environmental information
  • Maintain app stability and functionality

SunFlex does not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

User Responsibility

You acknowledge that sun exposure involves inherent risks and that individual skin response varies.

You are solely responsible for how you interpret and act on information provided by the App, including decisions related to sun exposure duration, timing, and protective measures such as sunscreen or clothing.

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information collected through the App. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Third-Party Services

The App may rely on third-party services (such as weather or UV data providers) to deliver information. These services operate independently and are governed by their own privacy policies.
